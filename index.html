About EXCAR

EXCAR stands for Ex-Cadets' Association of RUET. Ex-Cadets' Association of RUET (EXCAR) is the central hub for all the ex-cadets of RUET.It plays a heavy role in facilitating the development of communication and interaction among the old cadets in every sphere of life, assisting them in reaching a common goal that entails prospering as a community. Besides that, EXCAR ensures that we, the ex-cadets of RUET can always come to each other's aid whenever someone from our community requires it. One of the prime objectives of EXCAR is to bring everyone together under the same shade for different events throughout the year, leading to a perpetual state of harmony and connectivity.
